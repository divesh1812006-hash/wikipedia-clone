Elon Reeve Musk is a business magnate and entrepreneur. He is the founder, CEO, and chief engineer of SpaceX; CEO and product architect of Tesla, Inc.; and owner of X (formerly Twitter).

Early life

Musk was born in Pretoria, South Africa. He developed an interest in computing and technology at an early age and taught himself computer programming.

Career

Elon Musk moved to the United States to attend the University of Pennsylvania. He later co-founded Zip2 and X.com, which eventually became PayPal.

Companies

  • SpaceX – Aerospace manufacturer and space transport company
  • Tesla, Inc. – Electric vehicles and clean energy
  • Neuralink – Neurotechnology company
  • The Boring Company – Infrastructure and tunnel construction

Public image

Musk is known for his ambitious goals, controversial statements, and strong presence on social media. He has influenced discussions on space exploration, electric vehicles, and artificial intelligence.
